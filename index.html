NPS Calculator

Calculate your Net Promoter Score from survey responses. Paste data, upload CSV, or load sample data to see results instantly.

How NPS works: Respondents rate 0-10. Promoters (9-10) love your product. Passives (7-8) are satisfied but unenthusiastic. Detractors (0-6) are unhappy. NPS = % Promoters − % Detractors. Score ranges from −100 to +100.

Metric Glossary ▼ click to expand
Total Spend
Total ad budget spent on the campaign.
Impressions
Total number of times your ad was displayed.
Reach
Number of unique people who saw your ad.
Frequency
Average times each person saw your ad (Impressions ÷ Reach).
CPM
Cost per 1,000 impressions (Spend ÷ Impressions × 1,000).
Clicks
Total clicks on your ad.
CPC
Cost per click (Spend ÷ Clicks).
CTR
Click-through rate — percentage of impressions that became clicks.
Conversions
Desired actions taken (form fill, purchase, signup, etc.).
CVR
Conversion rate — percentage of clicks that converted on your landing page.
CPA
Cost per acquisition (Spend ÷ Conversions). How much each conversion costs you.
ACV
Annual Contract Value — average yearly revenue per B2B deal.
MQL
Marketing Qualified Lead — a lead that meets your marketing criteria.
SQL
Sales Qualified Lead — a lead vetted and accepted by the sales team.
Closed Won
Deals that converted to paying customers.
AOV
Average Order Value — revenue per transaction.
COGS
Cost of Goods Sold — direct cost to produce or deliver the product.
Profit Margin
Percentage of revenue retained after COGS.
LTV
Customer Lifetime Value — total revenue expected from one customer.
Churn Rate
Percentage of customers who stop buying each month.
LTV:CAC Ratio
Lifetime value vs. acquisition cost. Healthy is 3:1 or better.
ROI
Return on Investment: (Revenue − Spend) ÷ Spend × 100.
Breakeven
The point where ad revenue equals ad spend (ROI = 0%).

Exit Intent Popup

A drop-in script that shows a popup when visitors are about to leave your page. No plugins, no frameworks — pure HTML/CSS/JS.

What it does

  • Detects when a visitor moves their cursor toward the browser's address bar — the exit signal
  • Shows a customisable popup with a headline, message, and CTA button
  • Redirects visitors to any URL you choose
  • Built-in cooldown so the same visitor isn't shown it repeatedly
  • Silent on mobile — no hover intent on touchscreens

What you get

  • popup.js — the engine, drop it on any page
  • index.html — a full landing page mockup to demo it
  • admin.html — settings editor with live preview
  • readme.html — setup guide to share with your team
  • Zero dependencies, no npm, no build step

Growth tips

  • Offer something specific — a free guide or discount converts far better than a generic "subscribe"
  • Keep your headline under 8 words. Visitors are already leaving — you have 2 seconds
  • Match the popup offer to the page. A pricing page popup should address price objections
  • 24-hour cooldown is a good default — lower for high-traffic campaigns, raise for brand-sensitive audiences
1
Download popup.js and upload it to your web server Place it in the same folder as your HTML files, or a /js/ subfolder. Use the "Get the Code" tab to copy or download it.
